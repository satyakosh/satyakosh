# Contributing to Satyakosh

**Status: reconstructed; plain-English walkthrough. Founder to re-review.**

Thank you for helping build a treasury of truth. Here is how it works, in
plain words.

## Propose a fact
1. Read SCOPE.md first. Some categories are refused on purpose.
2. Open a pull request using the fact-proposal template.
3. Automated checks will run. They add flags; they never block review by
   themselves.
4. Your proposal enters the public review zone for at least 30 days.
5. If no objection stands at the end, the fact seals — forever.

## Catch an error
Finding a mistake is the most valuable contribution here. Open an issue.
During the Genesis Window, validated catches earn permanent inscription on
the chain itself.

## Privacy
On the chain you are an opaque ID (SK-USR-...). Linking your name to that
ID is opt-in, and you can delete the link at any time. The chain never
holds your name.
